# Getting Started

You do not adopt all of this on day one; the framework itself didn't. Pick your rung on the [graduation ladder](GRADUATION_LADDER.md) and start there. For most projects that means Rung 1, in an afternoon.

## Day one: the spirit

Write one page inside your repo stating the disciplines you are adopting:

1. **The human is sovereign.** Nothing is done, complete or fixed until they confirm it.
2. **Claims need receipts.** Agent output, scout reports and your own first pass are claims until checked against reality.
3. **Plans precede consequential work,** and a skipped plan is claimed aloud, never silent.
4. **Work lands in small validated increments** with a commit after each confirmed change.
5. **Working state is replaced coherently,** never appended to.
6. **Anything you intend to trust carries a pre-registered falsifier:** the result that would prove it worthless, written down before you rely on it.

That page is a working Memento adoption. Run it for a while before adding anything.

## When a trigger fires: found the estate

The field-observed triggers: state lost at a context reset that mattered · the same lesson relearned twice · an unreviewed claim relied on · concurrent work streams contaminating each other. When one fires, found `memento/` in your repo from [`framework/`](../framework/):

1. **Directives:** copy `framework/directives/CORE_DIRECTIVES_TEMPLATE.md` to `memento/protocols/CORE_DIRECTIVES.md`, fill the slots, write your fitting note.
2. **Playbooks:** copy the playbooks you will actually use to `memento/protocols/playbooks/` and fit them (planning and git operations first; the rest as their work arrives).
3. **Memory prosthesis:** copy the tier structure from `framework/memory-prosthesis/`, drop the `_TEMPLATE` suffixes, and write your first CURRENT_FOCUS.
4. **Conventions:** install the spine and the routing law into `memento/memory-prosthesis/active-knowledge/`, and give every estate file the frontmatter from birth. It costs one block per document and makes every later mechanism possible.
5. **Bank your founding memo:** a dated plan in the evidence archive recording what you installed, what you fitted, and what you deliberately declined. Your estate's first receipt.

## The operating rhythm

Per session: the restart protocol reads the surface (directives, CURRENT_FOCUS, STATUS) and reconciles against live files and git state · work proceeds in gated increments under the Active Playbook · substantive sessions bank frozen memos · before compaction, the pre-compact protocol graduates at most three learnings and redraws the working context clean.

## Mechanise only on evidence

Register the triggers that would earn each mechanism (`framework/conventions/TOOLING_TRIGGERS.md`) and build nothing until one fires. When you do build: pre-register the falsifier, witness the mechanism unconditionally, and document its death if it fails. The honest map of what mechanisation can and cannot yet hold is [`THE_ENFORCEMENT_SURFACE.md`](THE_ENFORCEMENT_SURFACE.md).
